HOW TO PLAY:

This is a point and click and puzzle resolution game with a humorous tone in which the user plays as a statue inspector.
You will have to closely inspect their documents to determine whether they are authentic or not. The statues you will analyze belong to the terracotta army, some which are trying to come back to their home, the emperor's tomb, after being stolen away. There are, however, many forgeries that will try to deceive you. With mechanics similar to "Papers, Please" and a chinese inspired aesthetic, you will have to closely inspect them to prevent any forgeries going into the emperor's sacred tomb.

1 - Compare the documents to the statue, making sure nothing is out of place

2 - Press the correct button to determine whether the statue can pass or not

3 - ???

4 - Profit (unless you fuck up)

There are three different ending depending on how happy the Emperor is with you.

The better you perform, the happier the emperor will be. In other words, do well or you won't like how this ends for you.
